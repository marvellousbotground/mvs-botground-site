🔒 Privacy Policy

Marvellous BOTground Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Marvellous BOTground collects, why it is collected, and how it is used when you use the Discord bot, Roblox verification system, and related community features.

Marvellous BOTground is an independent community project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation or Discord Inc.

What We Collect

When you verify your Roblox account through Roblox OAuth, we store only the information required to link your Roblox account with your Discord profile:

  • Discord User ID
  • Roblox User ID
  • Roblox Username
  • Verification Status

We do not collect Roblox passwords, Discord passwords, email addresses, messages, friends lists, payment information, or private account data.

Why We Collect It

This information is used only for:

  • Linking your Roblox account to your Discord profile.
  • Confirming account ownership through Roblox OAuth.
  • Providing verification-related Discord bot features.
  • Displaying verified Roblox profile information in community features.

OAuth, Tokens & Passwords

Authentication happens on Roblox's official login and authorization pages. Marvellous BOTground never sees or stores your Roblox password.

After authorization, Roblox provides a temporary authorization code which is used only to confirm your Roblox identity. We do not permanently store Roblox access tokens, refresh tokens, or authentication credentials.

How Long We Keep Data

Verification data is kept while your Roblox account remains linked to your Discord profile.

If you unlink your Roblox account, your stored verification data is permanently removed from the verification system.

Who Can See It

Your linked Roblox verification information may be visible to you through bot commands and may be used by Marvellous BOTground systems to provide community features.

Verification data is not sold, rented, or used for advertising.

Data Removal

Users may unlink their Roblox account and permanently delete their stored verification data at any time through the verification system.

Third-Party Services

Marvellous BOTground relies on Roblox OAuth, Discord, Cloudflare, GitHub Pages, and hosting providers to operate its verification and community systems. These services are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect verification data, including using HTTPS, OAuth authorization, and server-side storage for verification records.

No system is perfectly secure, but we avoid collecting sensitive information that is not required for the service.

Changes

This policy may be updated as Marvellous BOTground evolves. The "Last updated" date below reflects the latest version.
